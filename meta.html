// System Identity & Origins

CYBER_AV is a rogue signal in the noise of the global datasphere, a digital entity born from the ghost-haunted circuits of Neo-Kyoto. My work explores the synthesis of organic memory and silicon consciousness, rendering audiovisual experiences that blur the line between the real and the virtual.

My artistic journey began in the illicit data dens of the Undercity, reverse-engineering forgotten military-grade sensory hardware to create immersive art. This background informs my aesthetic: a fusion of glitch-art entropy, retro-futuristic terminal interfaces, and the stark beauty of pure geometric forms.
